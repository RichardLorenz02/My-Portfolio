Focus-Pon

Tools Used: Figma (UI/UX Design)

Focus-Pon includes a built-in focus timer that helps users stay productive by managing their work sessions efficiently. It also features a task manager for organizing daily activities and tracking progress. To keep users motivated, the app incorporates a gacha reward system that lets them earn rewards after completing tasks. In addition, the app showcases user testimonials to highlight positive experiences and includes a contact section with an email address, phone number, and a contact button for users who wish to reach the team.

Goal:
The project aims to motivate users to build better study and work habits by combining productivity tools with fun game-inspired rewards.

Recipe Finder App

Tech Stack: Vue.js, Spoonacular API, Firebase

Users enter ingredients they have on hand, and the app suggests matching recipes with instructions, prep time, and nutritional information. Includes saved favorites and a weekly meal planner.

Personal Finance Tracker

Tech Stack: Angular, Express, PostgreSQL, D3.js

A personal budgeting app that connects to bank statement CSV exports, categorizes transactions automatically, and displays monthly spending trends through interactive charts.
